Welcome to Amju Super Cool Pool!
To aim the cue, hold down the left mouse button and drag.
To take a shot, hold down the right mouse button and drag.. 
..pull back to increase power..
When you are ready, release the mouse button!
To move the cue contact position, drag the middle button.
You can use the arrow keys to look around..
..and Z and X to zoom in and out.
