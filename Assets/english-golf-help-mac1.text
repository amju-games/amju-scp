Welcome to Amju Super Cool Pool!
To aim the cue, hold down the mouse button and drag.
To take a shot, hold down Ctrl and the mouse button and drag.. 
..pull back to increase power..
When you are ready, release the mouse button!
To move the cue contact position, hold down Option..
..then press the mouse button and drag.
You can use the arrow keys to look around..
..and Z and X to zoom in and out.
